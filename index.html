Dear Sir,

There are teachers who do their job, and then there are those who go quietly beyond it — who stay invested in you long after the semester ends. You were always the latter. As I step out of college, I want to say something I have felt for a long time: thank you.

You brought data structures to life in a way that made me want to truly understand, not just pass. But what stayed with me even more was the encouragement — consistent, genuine, and always there when I needed it most. You believed in my goals, sometimes more loudly than I did myself.

And then, even after the semester was long over, you were still there. Helping me find my footing with GATE, giving your time without a second thought. That kind of care is rare. I did not take it for granted, even when I did not say so.

✦   ✦   ✦

Thank you, Sir — for being a teacher, a guide, and a well-wisher all at once. I carried your encouragement with me further than you know.

With all my gratitude and respect,
Vansika
